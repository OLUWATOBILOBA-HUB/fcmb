Top Online Safety Tips

  • Verify URLs: Always check the URL of a website before entering sensitive information.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Use 2FA for your online banking and other important accounts.
  • Do Not Click Suspicious Links: Avoid links in unsolicited emails or messages.
  • Update Your Software: Keep your operating system, browser, and antivirus software up-to-date.
  • Regularly update your banking password and use a unique one.
  • Verify websites: by checking for HTTPS and a valid SSL certificate.
  • Use Strong Passwords: Follow our password guidelines.
